Learn to Throw

Step by Step Video
  1. Center
    • Throw clay on wheel
    • Get clay wet
    • Wrap your hands around clay and center on wheel



  2. Open
    • Push down in middle of clay to form a hole
    • Widen the hole by pushing up against the walls



  3. Pull
    • Put even pressure against one wall of clay on either side
    • Slowly move your hands up walls of clay



  4. Shape
    • Push out or squeeze in clay to desired shape
    • Use potters knife, needle tool or potters rib to continue shaping
